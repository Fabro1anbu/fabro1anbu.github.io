Difference between learning and studying!!!

First and foremost thing you need to understand that learning and studying is not the same.

Posted by Fabro on March 26, 2021

Firstly you need to understand that learning and studying are not the same. Both are two different things but people always mess up with these two things and get lost somewhere. Before delving into this concept let me tell you the key difference between the two things, let's get started.

Study vs Learn

When you are reading something and memorizing it, this process of action is said to be studying you have familiar with this word when you were at school.

Let me take you to our school days. Back in our school days to learn something we used to study something that means we used to memorize something and we wrote exams that's why some students have forgotten those content which they had written in their exams after some months.

However, learning is not about studying, it's all about how you understanding the concept and implementing the concept. That's why it is impossible to study mathematics unless you were taking some sort of efforts to solve problems When you are making some efforts to implement something, instinctively this part of the process comes under the learning phase I hope you got some ideas about learning and studying.

How we can use studying to accelerate learning?

As for as you understand, you know how important to learn something rather than memorizing it. But studying also involve in the learning process not that much what I meant was if we are learning something the first process of learning was reading aka studying, once we get a clear view about these two things then we can easily identify two things.

Some people have the innate talent to learn something faster but I don't believe such things. If you need to learn something fast you need to have relentless hard work and smart enough to do implementing consistently.

An elephant never forgets

Consistent is a key here where you can progress little by doing the same thing again and again. This may cost you huge impact in your life if you are consistent enough to do it for five years from now on. It will give you a bigger impact on your life.
